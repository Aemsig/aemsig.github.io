About Carlotty

Transforming the parking industry with innovative smart parking solutions

Our Mission

At Carlotty, we are transforming the parking industry by creating affordable, scalable smart parking sensors that make urban mobility easier, more efficient, and environmentally friendly.

The Problem

Drivers waste an average of 17 hours per year searching for parking spaces, contributing to:

  • Increased urban congestion
  • Excessive fuel consumption
  • Unnecessary emissions

In Berlin alone, €112 million was generated in 2023 from parking fines, highlighting the urgent need for better solutions.

Our Solution

Carlotty's smart parking sensors combine cutting-edge hardware with flexible software to deliver precise, real-time occupancy data.
